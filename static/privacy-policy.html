Introduction

Welcome to MirrorPatternApp! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how our app handles your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We collect only the following personal information:

  • User Name: Your display name that you provide when setting up your profile

2. Photos and Media

MirrorPatternApp requires access to your device's camera and photo library to create mirror pattern designs. All photos and images are:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never uploaded to external servers
  • Under your complete control - you can delete them at any time

3. Device Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera Access: To capture photos for creating mirror patterns
  • Photo Library Access: To select existing photos for pattern creation
  • Storage Access: To save your created mirror pattern designs
Important: All permissions are optional and used only for the core functionality of creating and managing mirror pattern designs. You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To personalize your experience with your display name
  • To enable you to create mirror pattern designs from your photos
  • To save and manage your created designs locally on your device
  • To provide core app functionality and features

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

MirrorPatternApp is designed with privacy in mind. All your data, including your name, photos, and created designs, are stored exclusively on your device using secure local storage mechanisms.

  • We do NOT collect or transmit your personal data to any external servers
  • We do NOT share your information with third parties
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do NOT display advertisements or use ad networks

Your privacy is paramount: Since all data is stored locally, we have no access to your information, photos, or designs. Your data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

MirrorPatternApp does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Your data is never shared with external parties.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not specifically target children under the age of 13. However, since we do not collect any personal data beyond a display name (stored locally), and we do not transmit any data to external servers, the app can be used safely by users of all ages under parental supervision.

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible through the app interface
  • Modification: You can change your display name at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete your designs, photos, or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Permission Control: You can revoke camera and photo library permissions through device settings

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as the app is installed. When you uninstall MirrorPatternApp, all associated data stored by the app will be automatically removed from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

App Updates

When we release updates to MirrorPatternApp, we may introduce new features that require additional permissions. We will always request your consent before accessing new device capabilities, and you can choose to decline if you prefer.
